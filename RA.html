Research Areas

The SCIS has been carrying out advanced research and development in many frontier areas of Computing Sciences, Information Sciences and Artificial Intelligence and some of the broad areas of research are

  • Human Computer Interaction
  • Speech Synthesis and recognition
  • Data Mining
  • Parallel and Distributed Computing
  • Grid Computing and cloud computing
  • Software Engineering
  • Internet and Web Technologies
  • Network Security
  • Mobile Computing and Ad-hoc networks
  • Cryptography
  • Spoken Language Processing
  • Artificial intelligence and expert system
  • Computational Intelligence
  • Embedded Systems
  • Bioinformatics

Funded Research

The SCIS has attracted research projects from several agencies like DST, FIST, UGC, etc.

  • DST PURSE has provided massive financial support for establishing Embeded Systems Lab, Network and Forensics Lab and Computer Vision lab
  • FIST-II has provided financial support to set up Software Engineering, Spoken Language Processing and Network and Securityand wireless computing labs.
