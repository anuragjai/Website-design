The following is the summary of the criteria for admission into the various programmes:

  • Ph.D. admission is through an entrance exam followed by an interview. A JRF candidate or a GATE qualified M.Tech. student with 8.5 CPI or equivalent are exempt from the written test and may appear directly for the interview.
  • Admission will be based on JEE (Mains) score in order of merit will be the only criteria for admission (no entrance examination or any interview will be conducted).
    • M.Tech. admission is through GATE score only. Admission is done through counseling.
  • MCA admission is through the university entrance examination followed by counseling.
  • Foreign student admission to these programmes is based on TOEFL/GRE scores and academic record.

For more details on the admission to a specific programme, please refer to the corresponding programme page.
