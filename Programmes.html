Programmes of Study

The school offers five different programmes of study leading to: Ph.D. in Computer Science, five year Integrated M.Tech. in Computer Science, M.Tech. (Computer Science), M.Tech. (Artificial Intelligence), M.Tech. (Information Technology) with specialization in Banking Technology & Inform ation Security, in collaboration with IDRBT and M.C.A. In addition, the school also contributes and supports the M.Tech. (Computational Techniques) of School of Physics, 5-year Integrated M.A. and M.Sc. courses .
